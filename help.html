    Contact the New York State Department of Environmental Conservation (DEC)

    Here are some ways to contact the New York State DEC:

    • General Inquiries:

      You can visit the official DEC website for general information and frequently asked questions.

    • Contact Forms:

      The DEC website may have specific contact forms for different topics. Look for a "Contact Us" or "Help Center" section on their site.

    • Phone Numbers:

      For urgent matters or specific divisions, you may find phone numbers listed on their website. Please refer to the DEC's Contact Information page for a directory.

    • Email Addresses:

      Some divisions or programs may have specific email addresses. Check the relevant section of the DEC website for email contact information.

    • Mailing Address:

      New York State Department of Environmental Conservation
      625 Broadway
      Albany, NY 12233-1010

    Please note: It's always best to check the official NYS DEC website (www.dec.ny.gov) for the most up-to-date contact information as it may change.
