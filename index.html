What is deforestation?

Deforestation means that forests and wooded areas are cut down and removed permanently.

How you can explain deforestation?

Imagine a big park full of trees, where animals live and everything is green. Deforestation is like someone coming into that park and cutting down all the trees. And they don't plant new ones. So the park becomes empty of trees.
